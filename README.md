This report provides the complete analysis of the Universal Store Holdings Limited stock market during the fiscal year 2024-2025. This study uses different financial indicators like share price, trading volume, market capitalization, dividend yield, and price to earnings ratios to evaluate the company’s fundamentals and market sentiment. Similarly, this study evaluates different portfolios under different strategies, such as buy and hold, single trade, and multiple trade scenarios. Likewise different type of visualization was used so that it can gain the attention to important trends, irregularities and breakthrough
